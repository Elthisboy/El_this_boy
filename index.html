About Me

Who I am and what I do

I'm Cristopher, known online as El_this_boy — an independent Java developer from Chile specializing in Minecraft modding and custom gameplay systems. I build mods, maps, and technical tools using Fabric, Forge, and NeoForge, with a strong focus on cross-version compatibility and player experience.

My work ranges from utility mods and economy systems to narrative-driven projects and creative tools. I focus on writing clean, maintainable code, creating engaging game mechanics, and ensuring everything works seamlessly across platforms and Minecraft versions.

Whether it's developing a scanning tool, a tycoon economy system, or a VR-optimized map, I bring every project the same level of care and technical depth.
